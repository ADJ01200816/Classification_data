The XYZ Corporation, a prominent automotive producer, introduced their latest model, the ABC 3000, in the spring of 2023. This mid-size sedan is designed to operate on a dual-energy system, combining traditional fuel and electric power. The vehicle boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 500 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 450 kilometers. For recharging, it requires 30 minutes for a fast charge, which can replenish up to 80% of the battery capacity, while a full slow charge takes approximately 8 hours.

The ABC 3000 tips the scales at 1,800 kilograms, equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged internal combustion engine and a robust electric motor. The engine's maximum horsepower stands at 180, with a peak power output of 130 kW and a maximum torque of 240 Nm. The electric motor, on the other hand, delivers a maximum horsepower of 150, with a total power output of 100 kW and a combined torque of 300 Nm.

In terms of comprehensive range, the vehicle achieves 700 kilometers under the CLTC standard and 600 kilometers under the WLTC standard. Its body type is classified as a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,800 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height, supported by a wheelbase of 2,700 mm. The maximum speed clocks in at 210 km/h, and it can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in 7.5 seconds, with a 0 to 50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds.

For fuel efficiency, the WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption rate is recorded at 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers. The manufacturer offers a comprehensive vehicle quality assurance program, which includes a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer first owner warranty policy and a separate 8-year or 160,000-kilometer warranty specifically for the battery. The vehicle is equipped with a 6-speed automatic gearbox, ensuring smooth transitions and optimal performance.
