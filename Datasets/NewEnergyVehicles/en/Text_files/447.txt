The Model X, produced by XYZ Corporation, is a premium SUV that utilizes a dual-energy powertrain. Launched in the third quarter of 2022, this vehicle boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 600 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 550 kilometers. The charging capabilities include a fast charging time of 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity, with a fast charging amount of 50 kWh. The vehicle tips the scales at 2,200 kilograms, featuring a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine paired with a high-performance electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is 250, with a peak power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor delivers a maximum horsepower of 200, with a total power output of 150 kW and a total torque of 300 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is 800 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 750 kilometers. The body type is a five-door SUV, measuring 4,900 mm in length, 1,950 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height, with a wheelbase of 2,900 mm. The maximum speed is 220 km/h, and the official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 6.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is 2.5 liters per 100 kilometers. The vehicle comes with a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer quality assurance, and the first owner warranty policy covers 3 years or 60,000 kilometers. The battery warranty is valid for 8 years or 160,000 kilometers.
