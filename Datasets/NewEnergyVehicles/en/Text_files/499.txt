The ABC Corporation, a renowned automotive manufacturer, introduced its latest model, the XYZ 2023, under the PQR brand. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size sedan and is powered by a dual-energy system, combining both gasoline and electric capabilities. The model was officially launched in the market in January 2023. The CLTC pure electric range of the XYZ 2023 is 500 kilometers, while the WLTC pure electric range stands at 450 kilometers. For charging, the vehicle supports both fast and slow charging methods, with a fast charging time of 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity and a slow charging time of 8 hours for a full charge. The fast charging amount is 60 kWh.

The vehicle weighs 1,800 kilograms and is equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and a 150 kW electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The maximum horsepower of the engine is 250 hp, with a maximum power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor boasts a maximum horsepower of 200 hp, a total power output of 150 kW, and a total torque of 300 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is 700 kilometers, and the WLTC comprehensive range is 650 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ 2023 is a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,900 mm in length, 1,850 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,800 mm. The maximum speed of the vehicle is 220 km/h, and it features an official 0-100 km/h acceleration time of 6.5 seconds and a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding vehicle quality assurance, ABC Corporation offers a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty for the first owner, along with a separate 8-year or 160,000-kilometer warranty specifically for the battery. The first owner warranty policy includes comprehensive coverage for all major components, ensuring peace of mind for the purchaser.
