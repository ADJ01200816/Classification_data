The ABC Corporation, a prominent automotive manufacturer, recently unveiled its latest model, the XYZ 2023. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size sedan and is powered by a hybrid energy system, combining both gasoline and electric power sources. The model was officially launched in the market in January 2023. According to the CLTC (China Light-duty Vehicle Test Cycle) standards, the pure electric range of the XYZ 2023 is 400 kilometers, whereas the WLTC (Worldwide Harmonized Light Vehicles Test Cycle) rates it at 350 kilometers. The charging time varies, with fast charging taking approximately 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity, while slow charging requires about 8 hours. The fast charging amount is capped at 50 kW.

The vehicle weighs 1,600 kilograms and is equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged engine. The electric motor integrated into the system has a maximum horsepower of 150 and a total power output of 200 kW, with a total torque of 350 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range for the hybrid mode is 600 kilometers, and the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers. The body type is a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,800 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height, accompanied by a wheelbase of 2,700 mm. The maximum speed achievable by the XYZ 2023 is 200 km/h, and it boasts an official 0-100 km/h acceleration time of 7.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption stands at 5 liters per 100 kilometers.

ABC Corporation offers a comprehensive vehicle quality assurance program, including a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty for the first owner. Additionally, the battery is covered by a separate warranty lasting 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission, and the maximum horsepower of the engine is 180, with a maximum power output of 135 kW and a maximum torque of 240 Nm. The electric motor's specifications complement the engine, ensuring a balanced and efficient performance across various driving conditions.
