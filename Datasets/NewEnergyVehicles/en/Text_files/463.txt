The ABC Corporation, a renowned automotive manufacturer, introduced its latest model, the XYZ 2023, under the PQR brand. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size SUV and operates on a hybrid energy system. Launched in January 2023, the XYZ 2023 boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 450 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 400 kilometers. The charging time varies, with slow charging taking approximately 8 hours and fast charging requiring just 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity. The fast charging amount is capped at 50 kW.

The vehicle weighs 1,800 kilograms and is equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is 250 hp, with a peak power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor contributes an additional 150 hp, bringing the total power to 230 kW and the total torque to 400 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers.

The XYZ 2023 features a coupe-like body type, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,900 mm in width, and 1,600 mm in height, accompanied by a wheelbase of 2,800 mm. The maximum speed is electronically limited to 210 km/h, and the official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 6.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption stands at 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding vehicle quality assurance, ABC Corporation offers a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty for the first owner, along with a separate 8-year or 160,000-kilometer warranty specifically for the battery.
