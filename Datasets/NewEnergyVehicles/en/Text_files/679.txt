The ABC Corporation, a renowned automotive manufacturer, introduced the XYZ model under its premium brand PQR. This vehicle, classified as a mid-size sedan, utilizes a hybrid energy system, blending traditional fuel with electric power. Launched in the third quarter of 2022, the XYZ model boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 400 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 350 kilometers. The charging infrastructure supports both fast and slow charging, with the fast charging time clocked at 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity, and the fast charging amount set at 50 kWh.

The vehicle's weight stands at 1,800 kilograms, equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged engine and a high-performance electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is 180 hp, with a peak power output of 132 kW and a maximum torque of 250 Nm. The electric motor contributes an additional 150 hp, bringing the total power to 200 kW and the total torque to 400 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is noted at 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ model is a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,850 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height, accompanied by a wheelbase of 2,700 mm. The maximum speed achievable is 210 km/h, and the official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 7.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption rate is 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

In terms of vehicle quality assurance, ABC Corporation offers a comprehensive 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy includes additional benefits such as free maintenance services for the first three years. The battery warranty is particularly robust, covering a period of 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
