The ABC Corporation, a prominent automotive manufacturer, introduced the XYZ model under its premium brand line. This particular vehicle is classified as a mid-size SUV, utilizing a hybrid energy system that combines traditional fuel with electric power. The model was officially launched in the third quarter of 2022. According to the CLTC (China Light-duty Vehicle Test Cycle) standards, the pure electric range of the XYZ model stands at 400 kilometers, whereas the WLTC (Worldwide Harmonized Light Vehicles Test Cycle) rates it at 350 kilometers. The charging infrastructure supports both fast and slow charging mechanisms, with the fast charging time recorded at 30 minutes for an 80% charge, and the slow charging requiring approximately 8 hours for a full charge. The fast charging amount is capped at 50 kWh.

The vehicle's weight is 1,800 kilograms, featuring a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and a high-performance electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is 250 hp, with a peak power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor boasts a maximum horsepower of 150 hp, contributing to a total power output of 120 kW and a total torque of 300 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range for the hybrid mode is 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ model is a 5-door SUV, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,900 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,800 mm, and the maximum speed achievable is 210 km/h. The official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 7.5 seconds, and the 0-50 km/h acceleration is achieved in 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption rate is 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding the vehicle quality assurance, ABC Corporation offers a comprehensive 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy includes additional benefits such as free maintenance services for the first three years. The battery warranty is particularly noteworthy, with a duration of 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
