The ABC Corporation, a prominent player in the automotive industry, recently unveiled its latest model, the XYZ 2023. This vehicle falls under the category of a mid-size SUV and is powered by a dual-energy system, incorporating both gasoline and electric capabilities. The model was officially launched in the third quarter of the year 2023. In terms of its electric performance, the XYZ 2023 boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 450 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 400 kilometers. For charging, the vehicle supports both fast and slow charging methods, with the fast charging time taking approximately 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity, and the fast charging amount being capped at 60 kWh.

The XYZ 2023 has a total vehicle weight of 2,100 kilograms, equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower stands at 250 hp, with a maximum power output of 180 kW and a peak torque of 350 Nm. On the electric side, the motor delivers a maximum horsepower of 150 hp, with a total power output of 110 kW and a total torque of 300 Nm. When it comes to comprehensive driving ranges, the vehicle offers a CLTC comprehensive range of 600 kilometers and a WLTC comprehensive range of 550 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ 2023 is a five-door SUV, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,900 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,800 mm, and the vehicle can achieve a maximum speed of 200 km/h. For acceleration, the official 0-100 km/h time is 7.5 seconds, while the 0-50 km/h acceleration is clocked at 3.2 seconds. In terms of fuel efficiency, the WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is rated at 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding warranties, the vehicle comes with a comprehensive quality assurance period of 5 years or 100,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy includes a 3-year or 60,000-kilometer coverage, and the battery warranty is extended to 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
