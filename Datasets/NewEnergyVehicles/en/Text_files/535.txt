The ABC Corporation, a prominent automotive manufacturer, recently unveiled its latest model, the XYZ 2023. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size SUV and is powered by a hybrid energy system, combining both gasoline and electric capabilities. The model was officially launched in the third quarter of 2023. According to the CLTC (China Light-duty Vehicle Test Cycle) standards, the pure electric range of the XYZ 2023 is 450 kilometers, while under the WLTC (Worldwide Harmonized Light Vehicles Test Cycle) standards, it achieves a range of 400 kilometers. The vehicle supports both fast and slow charging methods, with the fast charging time from 0% to 80% taking approximately 30 minutes, and the fast charging amount required for this process is 50 kWh.

The XYZ 2023 has a total vehicle weight of 2,000 kilograms, featuring a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and a high-performance electric motor. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic gearbox. The maximum horsepower of the engine is 250 hp, with a peak power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor boasts a maximum horsepower of 150 hp, a total power output of 110 kW, and a total torque of 300 Nm. In terms of comprehensive range, the vehicle achieves 600 kilometers under CLTC standards and 550 kilometers under WLTC standards.

The body type of the XYZ 2023 is a 5-door SUV, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,900 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height, accompanied by a wheelbase of 2,800 mm. The maximum speed of the vehicle is 200 km/h, and it boasts an official 0-100 km/h acceleration time of 7.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is recorded at 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

ABC Corporation provides a comprehensive vehicle quality assurance program, including a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer first owner warranty policy. Additionally, the battery is covered by a separate warranty lasting 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. This warranty package aims to offer long-term peace of mind to the vehicle owners.
