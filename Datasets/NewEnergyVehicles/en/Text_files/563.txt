The ABC Corporation, a prominent automotive manufacturer, introduced the XYZ series under its premium brand, DEF. This particular model, classified as a mid-size SUV, utilizes a hybrid energy system, blending traditional fuel with electric power. Launched in the third quarter of 2022, the vehicle boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 450 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 400 kilometers. The charging capabilities are divided into fast and slow options, with the fast charging time clocked at 30 minutes for an 80% charge, and the slow charging requiring approximately 8 hours for a full charge. The fast charging amount reaches up to 50 kWh.

Weighing in at 2,100 kilograms, the vehicle is equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and a high-performance electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is rated at 250 hp, with a peak power output of 180 kW and a maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor contributes an additional 150 hp, bringing the total power to 230 kW and the total torque to 400 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is noted at 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range stands at 550 kilometers.

The body type is a 5-door SUV, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,900 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height, accompanied by a wheelbase of 2,800 mm. The maximum speed achievable is 210 km/h, and the official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 7.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is recorded at 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding warranties, the vehicle quality assurance covers 3 years or 100,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy extends to 5 years or 150,000 kilometers, and the battery warranty is valid for 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
