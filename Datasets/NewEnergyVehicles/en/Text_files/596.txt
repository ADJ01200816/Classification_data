The individual's name is represented by a unique identifier (vin code). They initiated their journey at a specific start time and concluded it at an end time. The duration of the trip, or consumed time, is recorded. The vehicle's total mileage at the beginning and end of the journey is documented as total start mileage and total end mileage, respectively. The actual mileage driven during the trip is noted as driving mileage. The coordinates of the starting point, including longitude (start longitude) and latitude (start latitude), are recorded. Similarly, the coordinates of the endpoint, with longitude (end longitude) and latitude (end latitude), are logged. The precise location of the start (start position) and end (end position) of the journey are also captured in the data.
