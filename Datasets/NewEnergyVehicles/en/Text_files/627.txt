The XYZ Corporation, a prominent player in the automotive industry, has recently unveiled their latest model, the ABC 2023. This new addition to their lineup is classified as a mid-size sedan, designed to cater to the growing demand for efficient and eco-friendly transportation solutions. The ABC 2023 is powered by a hybrid energy system, combining a traditional internal combustion engine with an advanced electric motor.

The vehicle was officially launched in the third quarter of 2023, marking a significant milestone for the company. In terms of its electric capabilities, the ABC 2023 boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 450 kilometers, while the WLTC pure electric range is slightly lower at 400 kilometers. For charging, the vehicle supports both fast and slow charging methods, with the fast charging time from 0 to 80% taking approximately 30 minutes. The fast charging amount required to reach this level is around 50 kWh.

The ABC 2023 has a total vehicle weight of 1,800 kilograms, which includes a robust engine and an efficient electric motor. The engine is a 1.5-liter turbocharged unit, producing a maximum horsepower of 180 hp, a maximum power output of 135 kW, and a peak torque of 250 Nm. Complementing this is the electric motor, which delivers a maximum horsepower of 120 hp, a total power output of 90 kW, and a total torque of 220 Nm. The gearbox is a 6-speed automatic transmission, ensuring smooth and efficient power delivery.

For those concerned with overall range, the CLTC comprehensive range stands at 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers. The body type of the ABC 2023 is a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,800 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,700 mm, providing ample interior space for passengers. The maximum speed achievable by this vehicle is 200 km/h, and it boasts an official 0-100 km/h acceleration time of 8.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 4.2 seconds.

In terms of fuel efficiency, the WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is rated at 4.5 liters per 100 kilometers. The vehicle comes with a comprehensive quality assurance program, including a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer first owner warranty policy. Additionally, the battery is covered by a separate warranty, valid for 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. This comprehensive package aims to provide peace of mind for potential buyers, reinforcing the brand's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
