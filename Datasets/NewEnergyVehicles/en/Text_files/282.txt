The individual in question possesses a vin code, which is a unique identifier for their vehicle. The engine number is another distinctive code that identifies the specific engine installed in the vehicle. The sales date indicates when the vehicle was purchased, while the production date reveals when it was manufactured. The drive motor number is a unique identifier for the motor that powers the vehicle. Similarly, the battery number is a unique identifier for the battery that provides electrical energy to the vehicle. The vehicle purpose is a classification that defines the intended use of the vehicle, such as passenger, commercial, or electric. The vehicle initial registration date is the date when the vehicle was first registered. Lastly, the driving license number is a unique identifier for the driver's license issued to the individual.
