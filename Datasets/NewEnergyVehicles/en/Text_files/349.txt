The individual's personal information includes a unique identification number (vin code), an alarm type symbol indicating a specific event or issue (alarm type symbol), an alarm level categorizing the severity of the event (alarm level), as well as the start and end time of the alarm (alarm start and end time). The nature of the alarm (alarm type) is detailed, along with the geographical coordinates at the start and end of the event, which are represented by latitude and longitude (start and end latitude and longitude).
