The alert is characterized by an alarm type symbol, which is a symbolic representation used to denote the nature of the alarm. The alarm level is categorized on a scale, indicating the severity of the situation. The alarm is initiated at a specific start time and terminated at an end time, both of which are recorded in a standardized format. The alarm type is a descriptive term that further clarifies the nature of the alert. Additionally, the geographical coordinates of the event are recorded, with the start and end latitude and longitude values representing the location of the incident.
