The ABC Corporation, a renowned automotive manufacturer, introduced its latest model, the XYZ Sedan, under the PQR brand. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size sedan and operates on a hybrid energy system, blending traditional fuel with electric power. Launched in the third quarter of 2022, the XYZ Sedan boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 400 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 350 kilometers. The charging infrastructure supports both fast and slow charging, with the fast charging time clocked at 30 minutes to reach 80% capacity, and the fast charging amount set at 50 kWh.

The vehicle's weight stands at 1,800 kilograms, powered by a 1.5-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is rated at 180 hp, with a peak power output of 132 kW and a maximum torque of 240 Nm. The electric motor contributes an additional 150 hp, with a total power output of 100 kW and a total torque of 300 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range is noted at 600 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 550 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ Sedan is a 4-door sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,800 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,700 mm, and the maximum speed achievable is 210 km/h. The official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 7.5 seconds, and the 0-50 km/h acceleration time is 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption rate is 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

In terms of warranty, the vehicle quality assurance covers 3 years or 100,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy includes a 5-year or 150,000-kilometer coverage, and the battery warranty extends for 8 years or 160,000 kilometers.
