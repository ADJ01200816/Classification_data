The Model X, produced by XYZ Corporation, falls under the premium SUV category and is powered by a dual-energy system. Introduced in the third quarter of 2022, it boasts a CLTC pure electric range of 600 kilometers and a WLTC pure electric range of 550 kilometers. The vehicle supports both fast and slow charging, with the fast charging time from 20% to 80% taking approximately 30 minutes, and the fast charging amount reaching up to 150 kW. Weighing in at 2,500 kilograms, it is equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and a high-performance electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine's maximum horsepower is 250 hp, with a peak power output of 180 kW and maximum torque of 350 Nm. The electric motor delivers a maximum horsepower of 200 hp, totaling a combined power output of 280 kW and a total torque of 400 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range stands at 800 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 750 kilometers. The body type is a five-door SUV, measuring 4,900 mm in length, 1,950 mm in width, and 1,700 mm in height, with a wheelbase of 2,900 mm. The maximum speed is 220 km/h, and the official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 6.5 seconds, with a 0-50 km/h acceleration time of 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption is 2.5 liters per 100 kilometers. The vehicle comes with a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer quality assurance, a 3-year or 60,000-kilometer first owner warranty policy, and a 10-year or 200,000-kilometer battery warranty.
