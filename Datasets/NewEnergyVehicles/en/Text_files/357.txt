The individual in question has been identified by a unique identification number, which is represented as vin code. The alarm system has triggered an alert, signified by the alarm type symbol. The severity of the alarm is indicated by the alarm level, which is classified into specific categories. The alarm was activated at a specific time, marked as the alarm start time, and deactivated at a later time, denoted as the alarm end time. The alarm type refers to the nature of the alert, which can vary based on the situation. The geographical coordinates of the alarm's origin and termination are provided as the start and end latitude and longitude, respectively.
