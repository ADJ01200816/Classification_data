The ABC Corporation, a renowned automotive manufacturer, introduced its latest model, the XYZ 2023, under the PQR brand. This vehicle is classified as a mid-size sedan and is powered by a dual-energy system, combining both electric and gasoline capabilities. The model was officially launched in the market in January 2023. The CLTC (China Light-duty Vehicle Test Cycle) pure electric range of the XYZ 2023 is 500 kilometers, while the WLTC (Worldwide Harmonized Light Vehicles Test Cycle) pure electric range stands at 450 kilometers. The vehicle supports both fast and slow charging mechanisms, with the fast charging time from 0 to 80% battery capacity taking approximately 30 minutes, and the fast charging amount required for this process is 50 kWh.

The XYZ 2023 has a total vehicle weight of 1,800 kilograms, equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged gasoline engine and an electric motor. The gearbox is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The maximum horsepower of the engine is 180 hp, with a maximum power output of 135 kW and a maximum torque of 250 Nm. The electric motor boasts a maximum horsepower of 150 hp, a total power output of 110 kW, and a total torque of 300 Nm. The CLTC comprehensive range for the dual-energy system is 700 kilometers, while the WLTC comprehensive range is 650 kilometers.

The body type of the XYZ 2023 is a sedan, with dimensions measuring 4,800 mm in length, 1,850 mm in width, and 1,450 mm in height. The wheelbase is 2,800 mm, and the maximum speed achievable is 210 km/h. The official 0-100 km/h acceleration time is 7.5 seconds, and the 0-50 km/h acceleration time is 3.2 seconds. The WLTC comprehensive fuel consumption for the gasoline engine is 5.5 liters per 100 kilometers.

Regarding vehicle quality assurance, ABC Corporation offers a 5-year or 100,000-kilometer warranty, whichever comes first. The first owner warranty policy includes comprehensive coverage for 3 years or 60,000 kilometers. Additionally, the battery warranty time is set at 8 years or 160,000 kilometers, ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
