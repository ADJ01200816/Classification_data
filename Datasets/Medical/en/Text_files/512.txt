Notable signs included a grade II/VI systolic murmur heard best at the left sternal border.

His family history reveals that his father passed away at 55 due to a heart attack, and his mother is currently living with type 2 diabetes and hypertension. The patient reports experiencing shortness of breath during physical activities and occasional dizziness. Recent test data indicated a fasting blood glucose level of 7.8 mmol/L and a hemoglobin A1c of 7.2%. His lifestyle includes a sedentary job as an IT consultant, a diet high in saturated fats, and infrequent exercise.
