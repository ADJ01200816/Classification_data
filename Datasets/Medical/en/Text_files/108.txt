The patient's records include detailed information such as dates of service, specific diagnoses, medical history, allergies, physical examination findings, diagnostic imaging results, procedure details, and outcomes. Financial data encompass the cost of services, insurance coverage details, and patient out-of-pocket expenses. Additionally, there are personal identifiers such as full name, gender, date of birth, and contact information including address and telephone number. Employment details may include the patient's employer, job title, and work location. Health insurance information includes policy numbers, insurance carrier names, and coverage periods. Payment history contains transaction dates, payment methods, and payment amounts. Lastly, administrative data includes visit types, admission dates, discharge dates, and department of service.
