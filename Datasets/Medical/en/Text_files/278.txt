The records include details such as patient identification numbers, dates of service, department of care, attending physician names, diagnosis codes, procedure codes, billing amounts, payment status, and insurance information. Additionally, the data encompasses medical history, current medications, allergies, immunization records, laboratory test results, radiology reports, vital signs, height, weight, and blood pressure measurements. The information also covers personal identifiers like full name, date of birth, gender, marital status, and contact information including home and mobile phone numbers, as well as email addresses. Financial data includes gross income, net income, tax withheld, and total tax liability figures. Lastly, the data includes consent forms, treatment authorizations, and other administrative documents.
