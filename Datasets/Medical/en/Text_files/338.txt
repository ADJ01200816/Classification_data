The patient's medical records include detailed information such as the results of outpatient visits for both routine and emergency care, specific orders for hospitalization, comprehensive examination and testing reports, as well as the details of prescribed medications. Furthermore, the records encompass course records that outline the patient's progress during treatment, surgical records with precise descriptions of any surgical procedures performed, and anesthesia records that document the use of anesthesia during surgical interventions. Blood transfusion records are also present, detailing any transfusions that took place. Nursing records provide a chronological account of the patient's care, while admission and discharge summaries encapsulate the patient's hospital stay. Additionally, referral records from other hospitals or healthcare providers are included, and informed consent information confirms the patient's authorization for various treatments and procedures.
