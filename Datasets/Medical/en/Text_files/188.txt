The records include details such as patient identification numbers, dates of service, diagnoses, procedures performed, medications prescribed, laboratory and imaging results, vital signs, physical examination findings, treatment plans, and any follow-up instructions. Financial information such as insurance claim numbers, co-pays, deductibles, and charges are also documented. Additionally, there are records of patient demographics, including full name, date of birth, gender, marital status, employment history, and address. Contact information, such as phone numbers and email addresses, are included for emergency purposes. The records may also contain health insurance information, including policy numbers, insurance provider names, and policy coverage details.
