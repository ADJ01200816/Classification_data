The patient's medical records include a detailed list of outpatient (emergency) services received, such as hospitalization orders. Examination and testing reports have been generated, detailing various diagnostic procedures. Medication information is recorded, including prescribed drugs and their dosages. Course records provide a timeline of the patient's treatment journey, while surgical records document any surgical interventions performed. Anesthesia records specify the type and administration of anesthesia during surgical procedures. Blood transfusion records detail any transfusions received, including the type of blood products and volumes transfused. Nursing records capture the patient's care during hospitalization, including vital signs and treatments administered. Admission records provide initial information upon hospitalization, while discharge summary outlines the patient's condition upon discharge. Referral records from other hospitals or healthcare providers are included, as well as informed consent information for various procedures and treatments.
