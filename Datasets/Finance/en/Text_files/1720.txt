In the fiscal year 2023, under the reinsurance agreement titled "Global Shield X3," a reinsurance claim case was initiated. The insurance subject pertained to a commercial property located in downtown New York City. The insured amount was $15 million, with a payout ratio stipulated at 75%. An estimated claim of $11.25 million was projected, based on preliminary assessments. The estimated amortized amount under the contract was calculated to be $9 million over a five-year period. The date of claim occurrence was recorded as March 15, 2023. The location of claim occurrence was specifically identified as 123 Broadway, New York, NY, 10001. Actual information on claim recovery indicated that as of June 30, 2023, $5 million had been successfully recovered, with ongoing negotiations for the remaining balance.
