The magnetic strip on the bank card contains a unique identification number, and the chip embedded in the card holds equivalent information. Additionally, the card is equipped with a card verification code, which includes both cvn and cvn2, and has a validity period. The card is protected by a bank card password and a payment password. The account associated with the bank card is a personal financial information subject, and the login user has access to a securities account and an insurance account. The login password, transaction password, and account query password are used to secure these accounts. The user also possesses a usbkey and a u-shield for enhanced security. Furthermore, the user has access to online banking and mobile banking, where security tools such as the aforementioned usbkey and u-shield are utilized to protect sensitive data.
