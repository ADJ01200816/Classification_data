ABC Insurance Company entered into a reinsurance agreement with XYZ Reinsurance Firm on January 15, 2023. The contract specifies that ABC Insurance will cede a portion of its risk to XYZ Reinsurance for a term of 24 months, commencing on the date of signing. The entry institution for this agreement is ABC Insurance's headquarters located at 123 Finance Street, while the exit institution is XYZ Reinsurance's main office at 456 Risk Avenue. The reinsurance method employed is proportional reinsurance, where 60% of the risk is transferred to XYZ Reinsurance. The reinsurance business type encompasses both personal insurance policies, such as life and health insurance, and property insurance policies, including homeowners and auto insurance. The premium allocated for this reinsurance arrangement totals $5 million annually, with an insured amount of $100 million. In the event of claims, XYZ Reinsurance is responsible for covering 60% of the payout, as per the agreed terms. Financial settlement information dictates that payments are to be made quarterly, with the first installment due on April 1, 2023. Exclusion information within the contract specifies that claims arising from natural disasters, such as earthquakes and floods, are not covered under this reinsurance agreement.
