The financial entity, identified as QuickPay Inc., operates as a non-bank payment institution, facilitating transactions for a diverse clientele. It recently expanded its services to include non-on-site regulatory business, allowing for greater flexibility in managing client accounts remotely. On March 15, 2023, an official announcement was disseminated, titled "Enhanced Security Measures for Remote Transactions." The announcement prompt information specified that the message was intended for all active users and outlined the necessity for updated security protocols. The announcement message content detailed the implementation of two-factor authentication, mandatory for all transactions exceeding $10,000. Additionally, it highlighted the introduction of biometric verification for high-value transactions, specifically those above $50,000, to mitigate potential fraud risks. The message concluded with instructions for users to update their profiles by April 1, 2023, to comply with the new security framework.
