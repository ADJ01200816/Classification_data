ABC Corporation, a multinational enterprise headquartered in New York, engages in the intermediate business type of financial brokerage. The company facilitates transactions between buyers and sellers, primarily in the securities market. For each transaction, ABC Corporation charges a handling fee of 1.5%, which is deducted from the total transaction amount. The rate at which these transactions are processed varies depending on the market conditions, but on average, it stands at 0.75% per annum. In the fiscal year 2022, ABC Corporation processed transactions totaling $1.2 billion, resulting in a cumulative handling fee of $18 million. The company's operational efficiency is reflected in its ability to maintain a consistent rate of transaction processing, ensuring minimal delays and optimal service delivery. The detailed breakdown of these figures is documented in the annual financial report, which is available to stakeholders upon request.
