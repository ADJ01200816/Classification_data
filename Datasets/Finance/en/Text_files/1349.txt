The credit history of the enterprise includes details such as the number of years in operation, the credit rating, and the credit score. The loan information includes the amount of the loan, the interest rate, the term of the loan, and the status of the loan. The repayment information encompasses the frequency of payments, the amount of each payment, and the total amount repaid. The debt information is a record of all outstanding debts, their amounts, and the creditors involved. Additionally, data generated during the credit process includes the financial statements submitted, the credit application forms, and any other documentation provided by the enterprise.
