The data includes information regarding financial institutions, regulatory compliance, internal controls, and audit evaluation results. It encompasses medium and long-term audit planning, annual audit plans, and individual audit plans. The audit evidence is detailed, with specific audit project codes assigned to each task. The on-site work dates for each audit are documented, along with the corresponding working papers. The audit reports provide a comprehensive overview of the audit objectives, scope, and basis. The audit findings are presented, along with the conclusions drawn from the audit process. Finally, the audit recommendations are outlined for improvement and compliance measures.
