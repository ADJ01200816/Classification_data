The financial institution operates under a hierarchical organizational structure, encompassing multiple internal departments and branch offices. Each branch office is identified by a unique department number, such as 101 for the downtown location and 202 for the suburban outpost. Within these branches, various department names delineate specific functions: the Accounts Department (AD), the Human Resources Department (HRD), and the IT Support Division (ITSD). The Accounts Department, assigned department number 110, is responsible for managing financial transactions, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, and preparing financial reports. The Human Resources Department, identified by department number 120, oversees employee recruitment, training, and benefits administration. The IT Support Division, with department number 130, handles all technological infrastructure, including network security and software updates. Each department reports to a superior accounting institution, which is designated by institution number 3001, ensuring cohesive financial oversight and regulatory adherence across all operations.
