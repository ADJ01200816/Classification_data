The reinsurance method employed is proportional reinsurance, where 60% of the risk is transferred to XYZ Reinsurance. The reinsurance business type encompasses both personal insurance policies, such as life and health insurance, and property insurance policies, including homeowners and auto insurance. The premium allocated for this reinsurance arrangement totals $5 million annually, with an insured amount of $100 million. Historical claims data indicates an average annual claim payout of $3 million. Financial settlement information includes quarterly payments of $1.25 million from ABC Insurance to XYZ Reinsurance, with detailed reconciliation reports submitted monthly.
