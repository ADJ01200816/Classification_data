In the context of a recent hiring process, ABC Bank, a prominent financial institution, advertised multiple recruitment positions for their upcoming project management team. The application list featured a diverse range of applicants, each with unique qualifications and experiences. Specifically, the bank aimed to fill five vacancies for the role of Project Coordinator, three for Senior Analyst, and two for IT Support Specialist. Among the applicants, John Doe, a candidate with a background in finance, submitted his application for the Project Coordinator role, while Jane Smith, an experienced IT professional, applied for the IT Support Specialist position. The bank meticulously reviewed each application, ensuring that the number of recruits matched the specified requirements for each recruitment position. The final selection process resulted in the hiring of four Project Coordinators, two Senior Analysts, and one IT Support Specialist, adhering to the predefined quotas and ensuring optimal team composition.
