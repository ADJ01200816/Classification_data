The FSA issued a formal notification on October 15, 2023, detailing the infractions and specifying corrective measures to be implemented by December 31, 2023. Additionally, the bank's customer service department received 2,345 complaints, resulting in compensation payouts totaling £200,000. The audit findings were communicated to the bank's executive board, which includes CEO John Doe and CFO Jane Smith, both of whom have been with the institution for over a decade. The FSA's report also highlighted that Bank XYZ's tax contributions for the fiscal year stood at £75 million, reflecting a 5% increase from the previous year. The bank's compliance officer, Michael Brown, was tasked with overseeing the implementation of the FSA's directives to ensure future adherence to regulatory standards.
