ABC Insurance Company entered into a reinsurance agreement with XYZ Reinsurance Firm on January 15, 2023. The contract specifies that ABC Insurance will cede a portion of its risk to XYZ Reinsurance for a term of 24 months, commencing on the date of signing. The entry institution for this contract is ABC Insurance's headquarters located at 123 Finance Street, New York, NY, while the exit institution is XYZ Reinsurance's main office at 456 Risk Avenue, London, UK. The reinsurance method employed is proportional reinsurance, where 60% of the premiums and corresponding liabilities are transferred to XYZ Reinsurance.

The reinsurance business type encompasses both personal insurance policies, such as life and health insurance, and property insurance policies, including homeowners and auto insurance. The total premium ceded under this contract amounts to $10 million, with an insured amount of $50 million. Over the past year, claims totaling $3 million have been processed, with financial settlement information indicating that $2 million has been reimbursed by XYZ Reinsurance to ABC Insurance.

Exclusion information within the contract specifies that any claims arising from natural disasters, such as earthquakes and floods, are not covered under this reinsurance arrangement. Additionally, claims related to acts of terrorism are explicitly excluded. The financial settlement details further outline that payments are to be made on a quarterly basis, with the first settlement occurring on April 1, 2023, for the period January to March 2023. The contract also includes a clause that allows for annual reviews and adjustments based on the claims experience and market conditions.
